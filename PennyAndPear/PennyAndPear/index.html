Penny & Pear can now deliver to you!

Unable to make it to our stores because of ailments and/or time? No problem! Penny & Pear is happy to finally unveil our delivery service option! All you need to do is fill out our delivery application online or in-store and we’ll have it processed within 1-3 business days!

Plant

When you plant, you grow so much more than just a crop, you grow life, energy, and renewal. So here at Penny & Pear, we extend this invitation to you, so that you may foster our planting beliefs into your work as well.

Produce

They say you reap what you sow, and here at Penny & Pear, we strive for our customers to sow the most affordable organic produce in Oregon. Our farmers are hard at work, from sunup to sundown, tending and caring for our best produce, nothing less than the best.
